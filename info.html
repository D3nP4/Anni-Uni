Awareness and Perspective

It is about creating awareness for different perspectives. Zooming in and zooming out more than overthinking. Looking from a different angle, because sometimes if you zoom out you see the whole picture and do not get stuck in a detail that does not work without context. At other times, you need to zoom in for a deeper understanding. It is a reminder to think about what you need. To think about yourself in the whole constellation. You are part of it. Movement comes from within. If you keep walking, you will see and experience different things. The way you see the sculpture now is different than how you saw it a minute ago, and different from how you will see it after the presentation, or in a year.

From Our Carrier Bag

Mental health moved us through the different approach to the person behind the design in this project. "How are you today?" is a simple question to follow up on each other. We wanted to take this further in our project. To get an idea of what is behind the design, the actions, and the person. What do they carry?

Architecture Context

Architecture is often only about the winning project. Performing under pressure and being present even after weeks of hard work and long nights. Many other study courses are similar, and often this creates a feeling of being against each other. A simple question can add a personal touch. It can create the feeling of being in this together and not being alone with a problem. It helps people find others to talk to and exchange with.

Translated Into Design

Three columns stand together. Each is individual, but all include one ongoing image. Perfect from one side, closed up and fitting. When you walk around and explore further, you notice openings and imperfections. We began to like this side even more. If you take time to go deeper, not only look from far away, they open up and show their inner beauty. They have a question for you, where you can open up, show some of your inner thoughts, and give space to chat and share something from your own carrier bag.
